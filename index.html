/Question & Answer

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've answered some of the most common ones below.

How long does a typical project take?
A typical bath remodel can take anywhere from 2 to 4 weeks depending on the scope of the project, custom materials, and any necessary plumbing changes. We provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
Do you provide free in-home estimates?
Yes, we offer completely free, no-obligation in-home estimates. One of our expert designers will visit your home to assess the space, listen to your goals, and provide an accurate quote.
What's your pricing structure?
We offer transparent, upfront pricing. The cost depends on the size of the bathroom, materials selected, and labor required. We have structured packages starting at $10,000 for standard updates, up to complete luxury remodels.
Do you handle all plumbing and electrical work?
Absolutely. We are a full-service remodeling company. Our team includes licensed plumbers, electricians, and tile artisans so you don't have to hire separate contractors.
